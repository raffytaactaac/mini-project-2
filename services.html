How To Purchase?

Hey RideXChange Community, these are the steps on how to purchase a 2nd hand cars. Please follow the steps below:


  • On the Car page, press "Test Drive" if you are interested to test and purchase the car.
  • Fill-up the form with precise Name, Email, Date, and Time.
  • Once you booked for schedule, just wait for the email via Gmail for confirming the schedule. (RideXChange Staff will inform you about confirming schedule)
  • Lastly, if your confirmation is scheduled you are free to go to test the car.

How To Sell?

Hey RideXChange Community, these are the steps on how to sell a 2nd hand cars. Please follow the steps below:


  • You need to create an Account and Log-in
  • On the Cars page you can see a dropdown with your name.
  • Click "Sell a car" then input the information about the car.
  • Once you're done with the informaton, automatically your car is added to the sell list of cars.

RideXChange is well known for showcasing 2nd hand cars that helps seller to have a platform, to advertise cars for potential customer.
